Projects

In the past years, I have worked on many topics in theoretical cosmology, with a focus on the computational and numerical aspects of the problems, which involved the modification and extension of old codes as well as the development and design of new software tools.
In particular, I am (have been) working on:

Constrained simulations of the local Universe (more details here), working on the generation of constrained Initial Conditions with the software tools Ginnungagap and IceCore (for which I have developed an MPI-parallel version).

N-body simulations of Coupled (scalar Field) Dark Energy models, extending my customized GADGET-2 version and developing a series of new tools (such as the MPI-parallel Merger Tree code). (PhD Thesis)

N-body simulations of Vector Dark Energy models, developing a modified version of the Tree-PM code GADGET-2. (PhD Thesis)

Parameter space constraints of Coupled Neutrino-Dark Energy models using the Boltzmann code CMBEASY. (Master Thesis)
