About me

I a postdoctoral fellow at the Racah Institute of Physics, Hebrew University in Jerusalem, Israel, since October 2014. I am working in the field of theoretical cosmology, specializing in N-body simulations.

I did my undergraduate studies and master at the Universita' degli Studi Roma 3 (Italy), while I obtained my PhD from the Universidad Autonoma de Madrid (Spain) in May 2014. I have also been a visiting graduate student at the Heidelberg University (Germany) and the Syndey University (Australia).

At the moment, I am a member of the CLUES collaboration, working on the reconstruction of the Initial Conditions of the local Universe from peculiar velocity data of galaxy groups and clusters.

For more informations please refer to my CV, the projects page of this webpage or my publications on ADS.
