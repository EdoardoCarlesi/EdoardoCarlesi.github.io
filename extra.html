Extra

I am the founding member and bass-payer of the Rome-based parody metal band Nanowar Of Steel, which is active since 2003 with more than a hundred of shows in Italy and Europe, four self-released albums and a live DVD. You can check us out on YouTube and on Facebook.

I also like foreign languages, besides Italian and English I am fluent in Spanish and Serbian; I also have a good command of German, French and Russian. At the moment, I am trying to learn Hebrew and Arabic (Shami and MSA).

In the spare time, I try to read as much as possible about economics and finance as well as history (breakup of Yugoslavia and SSSR in particular).
